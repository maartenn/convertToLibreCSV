Tidepool JSON to Freestyle Libre CSV

This tool will help you to convert your exported Tidepool data to the csv format used by Libreview. I created this website because my diabetes healthcare professional wants me to upload my cgm data in the Freestyle libre format but because I patched the app it doesn't send any data to the offical libreview anymore.

Additional notes

  • Use at your own risk!
  • Textarea might be slow when a huge amount of data is displayed, for copying the output to your clipboard you can use the copy-button
  • Your data will not be uploaded to my server! All conversion is done on your device and I won't have any of your data.
  • Be careful when using your clipboard for copy/paste and clean it whenever possible
  • I use the Dutch header in the CSV, most specific-made CSV parsers don't read the headers so I don't think this will be a problem.
  • I'm not associated with Abbot or Tidepool. I'm just trying to fill a gap between them.
  • Want to check or even improve the code? this website is opensource https://github.com/maartenn/convertToLibreCSV
  • If you have any questions / suggestions please let me know on github or contact me via email: mnaberink AT gmail.com
